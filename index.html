About <Grim>

Established in 2004, Grim is one of the original raiding Guilds of Daggerspine-US, reformed for Classic on Day 1. A mixture of friends both new and old; many from original Daggerspine guilds such as Unfortunate Consequences (UC) and Furniture That Kills (FTK), we are a 'Casually Competitive' raiding Guild, with a focus on efficient raiding and community. We are continuing our Classic adventures into Outland, and are currently recruiting raiders for TBC!

As a Guild, we have two primary goals:

  • To experience Classic WoW (now TBC) together, in a positive and mature environment, that keeps the enjoyable atmosphere, camaraderie, and spirit of Vanilla at the forefront.
  • To provide an efficient raiding environment, conducive to those with time constraints, that ultimately strives to conquer the entirety of endgame PvE content.

We are a mature, laid back group with an emphasis on long term collaborative play. As a collective we strive to make the most of our time raiding through self-improvement and cooperative strategy. We aim to try and get the most out of PvE raiding, while maintaining a reasonable life balance that doesn't require a large time commitment, and maximizing enjoyment.

Membership Application

We are currently recruiting for TBC!

We are not a hardcore Guild. We do not expect you to dedicate your life to raiding, we don't want you to pay to raid, and we don't have any crazy requirements. With that said, we have a certain level of expectation for all our raiders: other than attending regularly scheduled raids, we expect you to be self-sufficient, able to effectively pilot your class within a raid environment (improving when needed), and work towards the betterment of the raid.

For the most part, Grim is composed of laid-back adults with families, jobs, and responsibilities outside of the game, and we work best with players who share a similar mentality, or would like to play the game at that level of commitment.


Interested in joining? As of the TBC Launch, we are welcoming most interested parties to join us in our Outland adventures. Please get in touch an Officer, or fill out the form below (or click here) and we'll review it and respond as soon as possible.
